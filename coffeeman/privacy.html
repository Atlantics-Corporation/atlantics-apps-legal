Privacy Policy

Coffeeman — Grocery Tracker

Last Updated: February 21, 2026

Atlantics ("we," "us," or "our") operates the Grocery Tracker mobile application (the "App"), marketed under the brand name "Coffeeman." This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our App.

If you do not agree with the terms of this Privacy Policy, please do not access or use the App.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Personal Information You Provide

  • Account Information: Email address, full name, and password (for email-based registration).
  • Authentication Data: If you sign in using Google or Apple, we receive your name and email address from the respective provider. We do not receive or store your Google or Apple password.

1.2 Shopping and Usage Data

  • Shopping Trip Data: Trip dates, items purchased, quantities, units of measurement, product categories, and any notes you add.
  • Barcode Data: Product barcodes you scan using the App's barcode scanner (optional).
  • Item Patterns: Calculated purchase patterns, including average purchase frequency, average quantities, confidence scores, consumption rates, and predicted next purchase dates.

1.3 User Preferences

  • Notification preferences (enabled/disabled, preferred reminder time).
  • Prediction sensitivity settings.
  • Onboarding completion status.

1.4 Subscription Information

Subscription tier (free or premium) and purchase status managed through our payment processor (RevenueCat). We do not directly collect or store payment card information.

1.5 Information Collected Automatically

Device-Level Storage: We store limited data locally on your device using shared preferences (e.g., cached patterns for offline access, sync timestamps, onboarding state). This data does not leave your device unless synced to your account.

1.6 Information We Do NOT Collect

  • Location data: We do not request or collect your geographic location.
  • Contacts: We do not access your contacts or address book.
  • Browsing history: We do not track your browsing activity outside the App.
  • Analytics/tracking data: We do not use third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services.
  • Health data: We do not collect health or fitness information.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To provide and maintain the App: Process your shopping trips, calculate purchase patterns, and deliver personalized shopping predictions.
  • To create and manage your account: Authenticate your identity and maintain your user profile.
  • To deliver notifications: Send local reminders when items are predicted to be needed, based on your preferences.
  • To process subscriptions: Manage your subscription status and provide access to premium features.
  • To improve the App: Analyze aggregated, de-identified usage patterns to improve prediction algorithms and app functionality.
  • To communicate with you: Respond to your inquiries, provide customer support, and send service-related communications.
  • To enforce our terms: Detect, prevent, and address technical issues, fraud, or violations of our Terms of Service.

3. How Your Information Is Processed

3.1 Prediction Algorithm

Our App uses a Bayesian statistical model to analyze your shopping history and predict when you will need to repurchase items. This processing:

  • Runs on our secure server infrastructure.
  • Uses only your own shopping data — we never combine your data with other users' data for predictions.
  • Produces urgency classifications (critical, soon, stocked, learning) to help you plan shopping trips.

3.2 Voice Input

If you use the voice input feature to add items, speech recognition is processed locally on your device using your operating system's built-in speech recognition capabilities. Audio data is not transmitted to our servers.

5. Data Storage and Security

5.1 Storage Location

Your data is stored on Supabase's cloud infrastructure. Supabase uses industry-standard security measures, including encryption at rest and in transit.

5.2 Data Isolation

All database tables are protected by Row-Level Security (RLS) policies, ensuring that you can only access your own data. No other user can view, modify, or delete your information.

5.3 Security Measures

  • HTTPS/TLS encryption for all data transmitted between the App and our servers.
  • PKCE (Proof Key for Code Exchange) authentication flow for OAuth sign-in.
  • Row-Level Security policies on all database tables.
  • Secure token-based session management.
  • Password hashing and secure credential storage managed by Supabase Auth.

5.4 Security Limitations

While we strive to use commercially acceptable means to protect your personal information, no method of electronic transmission or storage is 100% secure. We cannot guarantee absolute security.

6. Data Retention

  • Account Data: We retain your personal information for as long as your account is active or as needed to provide you services.
  • Shopping History: Your shopping trip data and item patterns are retained indefinitely while your account is active, as they are essential for the App's prediction features.
  • After Account Deletion: When you delete your account, all associated personal data, shopping history, item patterns, preferences, and subscription records are permanently deleted from our servers within 30 days.
  • Local Data: Cached data stored on your device is cleared when you uninstall the App or clear the App's data through your device settings.
